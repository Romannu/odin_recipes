Tteokbokki

Description

Sweet and spicy recipe for one of my favorite south korean dishes.

Very easy to make at home, and it doesn't take a lot of time to do.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg | Tteokbokki
  • 2 | Eggs (2 p/person)
  • 2 | Fish balls (2 p/person)
  • Sauce
    • 3 tbsp | Gochujang
    • 3 tbsp | Sugar
    • 2 tbsp | Soy sauce
    • 3 tbsp | Agave
    • 4 tbsp | Ketchup
    • 2 tbsp | Honey
    • 50 g | Minced garlic

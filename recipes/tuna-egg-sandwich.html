Tuna Egg Sandwich

Description

Savory sandwich, perfect for snacking, or gym food.

Very easy to make at home, and it doesn't take a lot of time to do.

Ingredients

  • 1 | Eggs
  • 1 | Tuna Can
  • 2 | Bread Slices
  • 3 tbsp | Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Boil the egg for around 10 minutes (the quantity of eggs depends on how many sandwiches, it's 1 egg for 2 sandwiches);
  2. Get a bowl of cereals, and put the whole tuna in the tuna can, inside the bowl;
  3. Put 2 tbsp in the bowl, and mix everything really well, until there are no more "tuna clumps";
  4. Get the 2 slices of bread and spread the tuna-mayo mixture you just made on one side of one of the slices;
  5. Once the egg is finished boiling, cut it into slices, and put each slice on the bread slice;
  6. Close the sandwich with the remaining slice of bread;
  7. Enjoy your nutritious sandwich!

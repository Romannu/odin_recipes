Korean Fried Chicken

Description

Sweet and spicy recipe for one of my favorite south korean dishes.

Very easy to make at home, and it doesn't take a lot of time to do.

I used to eat this dish with my girlfriend in korea every week.

Ingredients

  • 2 | Chicken Breast
  • 3 | Eggs
  • Sauce
    • 2 tbsp | Gochujang
    • 2 tbsp | Sugar
    • 1 tbsp | Soy sauce
    • 2 tbsp | Agave
    • 3 tbsp | Ketchup
    • 1 tbsp | Honey
    • 50 g | Minced garlic
  • 1 kg | Flour
  • 1 kg | Panko bread
  • 1 l | Milk
  • 3 tbsp | Salt
  • 1 l | Frying oil

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken breasts in small, bite-sized pieces;
  2. Get a bowl and mix all the chicken breast pieces in the milk, eggs and salt;
  3. Prepare the sauce:
    1. Get a bowl;
    2. Put inside the bowl all the sauce ingredients, in no particular order;
    3. Mix everything with a spoon until it looks uniform;
    4. in this part, you can add portions as you see fit.
  4. Get a bowl and mix the flour with the panko;
  5. Dip the chicken breast pieces individually in the flour-panko mixture;
  6. Dip the chicken breast pieces in the frying oil - that should be at 180 degrees - until they get golden and crispy;
  7. Get a big pan and start boiling the sauce we made earlier, add water as you see fit;
  8. Once the sauce is bubbling, it is safe to insert all the fried chicken breast pieces, make sure all get the sauce;
  9. And that's it, you did it, you can serve! Enjoy!
